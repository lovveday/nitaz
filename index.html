Nitaz Official — 6 promotional video treatments and shot lists for outdoor hotel shoot, three videos per dress

Black dress · Video 1 of 3
"The dinner"
Anniversary dinner Hotel restaurant Fun + cinematic
Treatment
A couple's anniversary dinner at a fine hotel restaurant. Nitaz walks in wearing the black embellished dress. The room notices. A nearby husband cannot stop staring — his wife has to slap his hand to get his attention back. Nitaz's own husband pulls out her chair, his eyes fixed on her the entire time. The mood is warm, confident, and slightly playful. The dress is the reason the room shifts. The message: wear Nitaz and own every room you walk into.
Talent needed
Nitaz
Lead. Walks in, sits, commands the room. Confident, unbothered energy.
Nitaz's husband
Pulls out her chair. Admiring look throughout. Proud energy.
Prop couple (man)
Seated at nearby table. Eyes follow Nitaz. Gets slapped by wife. Comedic timing.
Prop couple (woman)
Notices her husband staring. Hand slap. Jealous but funny, not mean.
Props and setup
Dressed dining table — candles, wine glasses, plates
Small anniversary card or flowers on the table
Clear entrance walkway for Nitaz's arrival shot
Prop couple seated 1–2 tables away, visible in frame
Black dress · Video 2 of 3
"The birthday entrance"
Milestone birthday Hotel lobby or garden Cinematic
Treatment
It is someone's 40th. The party is already going. Then she arrives. No big announcement. Just the entrance — slow, deliberate, dress catching the light. Guests turn. Phones come out. A friend rushes to hug her and compliment the dress. No dialogue needed. The message: your birthday is a moment. Dress for it.
Talent needed
Nitaz
The birthday woman. Confident entrance. Warm smile when greeted. She knows she looks good.
2–3 background guests
Already in the space. React naturally — turn, look, pull out phones, nudge each other.
One close friend
Rushes to hug Nitaz. Can mouth "oh my god" or similar. Big reaction.
Props and setup
Subtle birthday decor — not over the top, just anchors the occasion
Guests with phones ready to "capture" the moment
Staircase or corridor entrance for dramatic arrival angle

"Getting ready"
Behind the moment Hotel room or corridor Cinematic
Treatment
No occasion named. Just the ritual of getting ready for one. Nitaz in the hotel room — dress laid out on the bed, she steps into it, zips up, looks in the mirror, and walks out the door. Close-ups of embellishment detail as she gets dressed. The final shot is her hand pressing the lift button — she is ready. The message: every special occasion starts with the dress. This is the dress.
Talent needed
Nitaz
Solo. Calm, intentional energy. No performance — just the quiet confidence of a woman who knows she is about to walk into a room.
Props and setup
Dress on bed or hanger before she puts it on
Full-length mirror for the "look" moment
Perfume, jewellery, bag on dressing table as atmosphere
Hotel corridor and lift button for final exit shot

"The garden reception"
Wedding guest Hotel garden or outdoor space Cinematic
Treatment
A wedding reception in the hotel garden. Nitaz is not the bride — she is the guest who steals the room. She moves through the space naturally — greeting people, laughing, holding a glass. The camera finds her the way guests' eyes do: effortlessly. The white dress against the outdoor light is the visual hook. The message: even as a guest, a Nitaz woman is unforgettable.
Talent needed
Nitaz
Wedding guest energy — warm, social, effortless. Not performing, just existing beautifully in the space.
2–3 background guests
Milling around, dressed up. React to Nitaz naturally — admiring glances, small compliments.
Props and setup
Champagne or mocktail glass in hand for one scene
Garden flowers or greenery as natural backdrop
Shoot in natural light — golden hour if possible

"The proposal dinner"
Life milestone Hotel restaurant Fun + cinematic
Treatment
A romantic dinner. The man reaches into his jacket pocket. She does not know what is coming. He gets on one knee. She covers her mouth — genuinely overwhelmed. Around them, the restaurant reacts. But the camera keeps coming back to her — her dress, her face, her moment. The message: for life's most unexpected beautiful moments, you should already be dressed for it.
Talent needed
Nitaz
The woman being proposed to. Genuine surprise and joy. This video lives or dies on her reaction.
Nitaz's husband
Plays the man proposing. Gets on one knee. Warm, loving energy.
Background diners
React to the proposal — clap, smile, take out phones.
Props and setup
Ring box — does not need a real ring, just needs to look the part
Romantic table setting — candles, roses, wine
Intimate corner table so the scene feels private but visible
White dress · Video 3 of 3
"She walked in and the room knew"
Brand film Full hotel — lobby, garden, restaurant Cinematic
Treatment
This is the brand film. No specific occasion — all occasions. Nitaz moves through different spaces of the hotel: the lobby, the garden, the restaurant. In each space, people notice. The edit is fast-paced and confident, cut to music. No dialogue. Ends on a single line on screen: "Timeless elegance. Nitaz." This is the video that goes on the profile, on the website, everywhere. It is the identity film for the white dress.
Talent needed
Nitaz
Pure presence. Three different spaces, three different micro-moments. Same dress, same energy throughout.
Background talent
Different people in each location reacting differently — admiration, double-take, whisper to a friend.
Props and setup
Hotel lobby — clean, grand entrance shot
Garden — movement shot, natural light
Restaurant — seated, composed, commanding
End card: "Timeless elegance. Nitaz." on screen
